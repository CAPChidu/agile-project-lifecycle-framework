## ğŸ’¡ Usage Examples

### Sprint Planning Session
```markdown
1. Review product backlog with stakeholders
2. Use sprint-planning.md template to structure the meeting
3. Apply risk-assessment-matrix for high-priority items
4. Update velocity-tracker with team capacity
5. Document sprint goals and acceptance criteria
```

### Risk Identification Workflow
```markdown
1. Conduct risk brainstorming with team
2. Log identified risks in risk-register-template.xlsx
3. Assess probability and impact using risk-assessment-matrix
4. Assign risk owners and mitigation strategies
5. Schedule regular risk review meetings
```

## ğŸ“ Best Practices

### For Scrum Masters
- Facilitate ceremonies using provided templates
- Track team velocity and adjust sprint capacity accordingly
- Maintain transparent communication with stakeholders
- Continuously refine the backlog with product owner

### For Project Managers
- Integrate risk management into sprint planning
- Use stakeholder analysis to prioritize engagement
- Monitor KPIs through automated dashboards
- Document lessons learned for continuous improvement

### For Development Teams
- Follow Definition of Done (DoD) guidelines
- Update task status in real-time for accurate tracking
- Participate actively in retrospectives
- Collaborate on risk mitigation strategies
